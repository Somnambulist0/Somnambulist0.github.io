I am Jingcheng Ni, currently a junior student in Duke Kunshan University. I have a deep interest in the field of computer vision, but as an undergraduate, I am equally excited about the other tracks except the computer vision, especially the interdisciplinary potential of combining computer vision with insights from other fields.

My academic background includes courses in Python, Discrete Mathematics, Multivariable Calculus, Linear Algebra, and Probability Theory. I have also enrolled the Machine Learning this semester. Beyond my formal education, I have self-studied Deep Learning and PyTorch. My prior research experience includes work on a project involving Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) for novel view synthesis with sparse views. This project equipped me with a basic understanding of deep learning and computer vision.
